Our Strategic Plan

For nearly 40 years, Boulder County Farmers Markets has invited neighbors to enjoy delicious, local food and uplift Colorado agriculture. Yet, right here in our very own community, the future of farming is uncertain. To create a world where local food and farmers can thrive, we’ve created a Strategic Plan that offers a bold reimagining of our work as we continue to collectively transform our food system — one bite at a time.
